About me

My name is Mariana Danielová and I come from the Czech Republic. I was born in Prague but in October 2012 I moved to Munich and currently I am living in Vienna where I am studying international Master of Cartography.

I really like every kind of maps and everything connected to them. That is why I became a member of non profit organization called Juzit three years ago. This organization is creating special maps for young travellers and it is only a small Czech part of large European project called USE-IT Europe. As a member of this organization I made the map data and map design for maps USE-IT Prague 2011 and USE-IT Prague 2012.
