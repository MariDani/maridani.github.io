Design Contest


First place in Gravity Design Contest 2017 - contest of snowboard design

QGIS + Inkscape + Gimp + OSM data


I was trying to connect two things that I like: cartography and snowboarding. When someone says the word “snowboard”, I imagine mountains, as all other riders. One of my favorite ski resorts is Les Trois Vallées in France. I have the best memories for freeriding from this area.

The top part of the board covers one of the 3D model visualizations (TIN) showing part of this ski resort. In the middle of the board (between the bindings), there is mountain Mont Vallon, from which you can choose between many freerides with small bumps and a few pine trees in the bottom. Next I added layers with glaciers (blue areas) and forests (green areas). These layers are visualized according to reality, the source of the data is OSM. The base layer is a visualization of contour lines. Green and blue colors indicate places with lower altitudes so it is easy to recognize how the terrain is dividing into the three valleys.
